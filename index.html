About App

This application works with the spreadsheet by giving you live and mobile-friendly looks at your spreadsheet, wherever you are. It shows the breakdown of your assets and current asset distribution with deltas to the target allocation.

Mobile-friendly

The application has been designed from the ground up to be mobile-friendly. No more clunky Google Sheet that you must zoom in-and-out again and again.

Stocks, ETFs, Crypto, and Managed funds

You can follow your live status of your assets and check their balance according to your investment plan.

EU, US, UK & AUS Support

You can be user of any of the latest sheet variants. This application can read any regional variant of the sheet as long as the sheet is the complete edition in the first place. You can change currency formating via settings screen.

How The App Works?

Sign in with Google Account

You must log in to the application using your Google account.

Give permissions

The application requires read-only permissions for your drive files and sheets in order to read the information that will be shown in the application. To be exact:

  • See information about your Google Drive files: Required to fetch list of spreadsheets.
  • See all your Google Sheets spreadsheets: To read information from the selected spreadsheet to the dashboard and other application functionalities.

Select sheet

In the first time use, you must select your copy of Compile Sanity Personal Finance template. The application will remember your selection and you can change selection via settings screen.

Enjoy

All of these steps must be done at the first time of use, after that the application is ready to use.
